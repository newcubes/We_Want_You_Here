Stories



The courage and generosity of the women who share their stories here is greatly appreciated. Through reading and responding to them, we all become wiser.


These stories are grouped by themes. Each interview is book-ended by “Encounter”--my first impressions and our conversation--followed by “Drive Home,” which offers my reflections, questions, and connections after re-listening to our interview multiple times, even years later.
